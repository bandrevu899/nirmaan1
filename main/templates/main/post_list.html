NGO CONFERENCE

Different NGOs come to Bits-pilani,Hyderabad campus which provides a common platform for them to interact
Each organisation is given a chance to share their activities and ideas
These ideas are exchanged among the organisations and discussions happen on how to implement them.
Gives a platform to discuss on how different organisations can come together to further their reach.

CAMPUS FESTS

In a campus of 4000 students small iniatives and leave a big impact
Blood donation events happen during the course of the whole year where students are encouraged to donate blood
A collection drive where students give away different items which they no longer use and are distributed to the people who need it
Some things are useless to some people while they are valuables to others
Selling of candles and colors that were made by unemployed women during different festivals like diwali and holi and the money is given back to them

IGNITE

650 underpriviledged orphans come to Bits-pilani,Hyderabad to get inspired by the students of the campus
A 2 day continous event where the children are taught different values and morals that are essential in life.
